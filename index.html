OUR work process

How our services work

Initial Discussion (1 hour conversation - no fee)

An initial discussion with School Leader(s) to present the program and answer questions.

  • Describe the process, benefits, time commitments, program options, risk management, strategy and costs.
  • Assessment of existing teacher(s) and school capabilities, requirements and constraints for potential fit.
  • Identify specific concerns that could include items such as teacher retention, quality assurance, timing etc.
Holistic Assessment

An information gathering workshop to capture all relevant information required to develop the uplift program.

  • Participants: Teacher(s) and, optionally, School Leaders
  • 1x4 hour or 2x2 hour workshops

Focus areas:

  • Knowledge across relevant technology domains
  • Breadth of teaching experience
  • Program development experience
  • Specific school custom and practice
  • Constraints such as timeline and teacher workload
